Get Involved with AdaptBTC

AdaptBTC is a community-driven educational project. Whether you're a beginner or a Bitcoin veteran, there are many ways to contribute, learn, and support the mission.

Ways You Can Participate

Spread the Word

Share AdaptBTC resources, pages, and tools with friends, family, or coworkers who want to learn about Bitcoin.

Join Our Newsletter

Get updates, educational material, and announcements directly to your inbox. Built for beginners and long-term thinkers.

Suggest Improvements

If you spot something confusing or think we should add a feature, tool, or page — tell us.

Partner with Us

Small businesses, groups, and organizations can collaborate for education, workshops, or Bitcoin onboarding.

Contribute Skills

Designers, developers, educators, and BTC enthusiasts can help expand AdaptBTC’s content and tools.

Support the Mission

Donations help fund free education and development. Every contribution helps grow the project.

Be Part of a Growing Community

Bitcoin education is for everyone — and together, we can make it more accessible.
